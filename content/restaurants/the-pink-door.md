# The Pink Door

## A Hidden Treasure

Tucked away behind a bright pink door in Pike Place Market, The Pink Door has been serving authentic Italian cuisine since 1981. Our cozy, eclectic space features vintage decor, live music, and a menu that celebrates the best of Italian cooking.

## Culinary Highlights

- Fresh handmade pastas daily
- Wood-fired pizzas with seasonal toppings
- Fresh Pacific Northwest seafood
- Classic Italian desserts
- Extensive wine and cocktail list

## Unique Experience

Our cabaret shows add a touch of entertainment to your dining experience, making every visit memorable. The pink door tradition dates back to Prohibition-era speakeasies.

## What Makes Us Special

- All pasta made fresh daily
- Local, seasonal ingredients
- Intimate, lively atmosphere
- Live entertainment most nights
