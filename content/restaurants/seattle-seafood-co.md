# The Seattle Seafood Co.

Located in the heart of Pike Place Market, The Seattle Seafood Co. has been serving the freshest Pacific Northwest seafood since 1985. Our commitment to quality and sustainability makes us a favorite among locals and tourists alike.

- Dungeness Crab
- Local Oysters
