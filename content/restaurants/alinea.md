# Alinea

## Culinary Innovation

Since opening in 2005, Alinea has redefined fine dining in Chicago and around the world. Our multi-course tasting menus feature innovative techniques and presentations that have influenced chefs globally.

Each Alinea experience is unique, featuring 12-24 courses that explore texture, flavor, and presentation in unprecedented ways. Our team creates custom dining experiences for each guest.
