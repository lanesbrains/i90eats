# Blue Island Oyster Bar & Seafood

## Chicago's Seafood Tradition

Established in 1982, Blue Island Oyster Bar has been Chicago's go-to destination for exceptional seafood. Our focus on fresh, sustainable seafood and warm hospitality has made us an Old Town institution.

## Fresh Seafood Daily

- Over 20 varieties of oysters
- Fresh fish and shellfish
- Sustainable seafood sourcing
- Daily seafood specials
- Raw bar selections

## Menu Highlights

- Oyster platters (6, 12, 18 piece)
- Seafood towers
- Fresh fish preparations
- Classic seafood dishes
- Extensive wine and beer selection

## Atmosphere

Our casual yet sophisticated space features a lively raw bar, comfortable booths, and an energetic atmosphere perfect for celebrations or casual dining.

## Sustainability

We partner with local fishermen and prioritize sustainable harvesting practices to ensure the freshest, most responsible seafood available.
