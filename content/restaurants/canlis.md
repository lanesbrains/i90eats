# Canlis

## About Us

Founded in 1950, Canlis has been a cornerstone of fine dining in Seattle, renowned for our Pacific Northwest cuisine and breathtaking views of Lake Union. We pride ourselves on using the freshest local ingredients and creating memorable dining experiences.

## Signature Dishes

- Pacific Northwest Seafood Collection
- Roasted Marrow Bones with Seasonal Relish
- Duck with Cherries and Duck Liver Mousse
- Halibut with Shellfish and Fennel
- Famous Canlis Salad

## Atmosphere

Our award-winning restaurant features floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking Lake Union, creating an elegant yet approachable atmosphere perfect for special occasions or romantic dinners.
