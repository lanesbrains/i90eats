# Best Pizza in Missoula Along I‑90: Slices That Actually Live Up to the Hype

Missoula’s pizza scene is sneaky good—like “why is this in Montana?” good. If you’re cruising I‑90 and craving carbs, trust me, these spots won’t disappoint.

## 1. Biga Pizza — The Undisputed Champ

Locals love it, visitors rave about it, and yes, it’s worth the wait.  
**Must‑try:** Flathead cherry & sausage pizza. Sweet + savory fire.

## 2. Bridge Pizza — College‑Town Classic

Affordable, fast, and way better than it has any right to be.  
**Must‑try:** Their pesto slices.

## 3. The Pie Hole — Late‑Night Legends

If you're rolling through town after midnight, Pie Hole is the move.  
**Must‑try:** Potato bacon slice. Don’t question it.

## The Verdict

Missoula might not be NYC, but it’s carving out its own lane with creative, reliable pies that slap.
