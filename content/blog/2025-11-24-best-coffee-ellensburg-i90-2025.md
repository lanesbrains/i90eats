# Best Coffee in Ellensburg Along I‑90: Caffeine Stops That Actually Wake You Up

Ellensburg is one of those towns where the coffee is way better than you expect—especially if you know where to look.

## 1. D&M Coffee — Local Icon

Roasted in‑house, always fresh, zero misses.  
**Must‑try:** Their house espresso.

## 2. The Early Bird — Cozy, Warm, Always Busy

This place feels like Ellensburg distilled into a café.  
**Must‑try:** Honey lavender latte.

## 3. Winegars Coffee & Creamery — Two Words: Affogato Heaven

Yeah, it’s a coffee shop AND an ice‑cream spot.  
**Must‑try:** Affogato with huckleberry ice cream.

## Why Ellensburg Coffee Hits Hard

Maybe it’s the student energy, maybe it’s the small‑town charm, but these spots prove that great coffee is absolutely worth the detour.
