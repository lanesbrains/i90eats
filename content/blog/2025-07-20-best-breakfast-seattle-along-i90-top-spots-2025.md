## Top Breakfast Spots in Seattle

1. **Le Pigeon** - French-inspired eggs Benedict with truffle hollandaise—decadent AF. Reddit: "Worth the hype." Capitol Hill, quick from I-90. Price: $14-18. Dry humor: So fancy, your sweatpants feel underdressed.

2. **Portage Bay Cafe** - Organic buffet-style: build-your-own with local fruits. X: "Healthy but hearty." Fremont detour. Price: $12-16. Fun fact: Compost everything—eco-king approved.

3. **Lady Jaye** - Cornbread French toast, savory-sweet bliss. Redditors: "Brunch boss." Near I-90 south. Price: $11-15. Chuckle: Sticks to ribs like bad ex advice.

4. **The Wandering Goose** - Southern-fried chicken and waffles. Price: $13-17.

5. **Lola** - Mediterranean shakshuka, spicy kick. Wrap-up: Global mornings.
