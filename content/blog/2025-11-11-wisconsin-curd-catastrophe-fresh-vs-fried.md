### The Science of the Squeak

The squeak is caused by the elastic protein fibers in the young cheese rubbing against the enamel of the teeth. This elasticity is lost rapidly as the curd ages or is heated. Therefore, a fried curd is, by definition, an inferior product to the traditionalists.

This is the communal, comforting, tailgate food that defines the Midwest bar scene.

The Fried Curd fan base argues that the deep-fry method is an elevation, turning a simple, somewhat bland snack into the ultimate, savory comfort food, especially when paired with a cold beer.
