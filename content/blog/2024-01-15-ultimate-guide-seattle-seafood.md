# The Ultimate Guide to Seattle's Best Seafood Spots

Seattle's location on Puget Sound makes it a seafood lover's paradise. From the iconic Pike Place Fish Market to hidden gems tucked away in neighborhoods, the Emerald City offers some of the best seafood dining in the Pacific Northwest.

## Pike Place Market Area

### The Seattle Seafood Co.

Located right in Pike Place Market, this restaurant has been serving fresh Pacific Northwest seafood since 1985. Their dungeness crab and salmon are must-tries.

**What to Order:**

- Fresh Pacific Salmon
- Dungeness Crab
- Local Oysters

## Waterfront Dining

The Seattle waterfront offers stunning views paired with exceptional seafood. Many restaurants here source their fish directly from local fishermen.

### Pro Tips for Seafood Dining in Seattle

1. **Ask about the catch of the day** - Seattle restaurants pride themselves on fresh, local catches
2. **Try the oysters** - Washington state produces some of the world's best oysters
3. **Don't miss the salmon** - Pacific Northwest salmon is legendary for good reason
4. **Pair with local wine** - Washington state wines complement seafood perfectly

## Best Times to Visit

- **Happy Hour**: Many seafood restaurants offer discounted oysters and appetizers
- **Lunch**: Often less crowded with the same great food
- **Summer**: Peak season for fresh local seafood

Seattle's seafood scene is constantly evolving, with new restaurants opening and established favorites continuing to innovate. Whether you're a local or just visiting, these spots will give you a true taste of the Pacific Northwest's incredible seafood culture.
