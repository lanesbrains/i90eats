## Top 5 Pizza Places in Spokane

1. **The Flying Goat** - This spot's a legend on Reddit for its wood-fired pies. Try the "Goat" pizza with prosciutto and arugula—crispy crust, fresh greens, and a tang that'll wake you up better than truck-stop coffee. Reviews say it's "legitimately good" without the pretension. Location: Garland District, easy off I-90 via Division Street exit. Price: $18-22. Dry humor alert: If your pizza doesn't arrive looking like art, send it back—life's too short for boring slices. Pair it with their garlic aioli; one Redditor swears it changes lives.

2. **DiNardi's Pizza & Pasta** - Old-school vibes here, with thick-crust that's hearty enough for a long haul. X users love the pepperoni—charred edges, gooey cheese. One post called it "garbage garbage" from a rival fan, but the consensus? Solid, no-frills winner. Near downtown, quick exit via Monroe Street. Price: $15-20. Fun fact: They've been slinging pies since the '80s, outlasting bad hair trends.

3. **Bennidito's Pizza** - Die-hard fans on Reddit swear by the thick-crust loaded with toppings. Go for the "Bennidito's Special" with sausage and peppers—juicy, flavorful, and portioned like they know you're road-weary. Off I-90 via Division St. Price: $16-24. Chuckle: It's so filling, you might skip dinner... or not, who am I kidding?

4. **Market Street Pizza** - Stylish spot with creative twists, like veggie-loaded options that even carnivores dig. X mentions highlight the "incredible crust." South Perry District, short detour off I-90 east. Price: $14-22. Their root beer floats are a hidden gem, per Reddit.

5. **South Perry Pizza** - Changing menus keep it fresh. Try the seasonal specials—wood-fired with local ingredients. Cozy vibe, great for a break. South Hill area, via Grand Boulevard exit. Price: $17-23. Wrap-up: If pizza's your vice, this list'll make your I-90 trek epic.
