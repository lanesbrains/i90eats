## Top Breakfast Spots in Missoula

1. **Paul's Pancake Parlor** - Reddit's top pick for sourdough pancakes: fluffy, tangy, and "outstanding." Huge menu—add bacon or fruit, or go for omelets with local cheese. Open since 1964, they've perfected fermentation. Near I-90 west via Orange Street. Price: $10-14. Humor: These stacks are so tall, they might block your GPS signal. Try the huckleberry syrup—Montana's berry gold.

2. **River City Grill** - Diner food done right, open for breakfast/lunch. Try the specials—eggs, hash browns, endless coffee. Reddit loves the portions: "Awesome for any meal." Family-owned since the '90s. Bonner exit off I-90. Price: $9-13. Dry wit: Their coffee refills are infinite, like your regrets from skipping breakfast.

3. **Burns St. Bistro** - Brunch vibes with fresh twists. Eggs Benedict or burritos that "rock." X: "Only brunch I like." Established 2012, local farm sourcing. Downtown via Higgins Avenue. Price: $12-16. Chuckle: Fancy enough for photos, but filling like a trucker's plate.

4. **Ninja Mike's** - Breakfast sandwiches that'll make you a believer. "Best in Missoula," per Hank Green on X. In-house biscuits, sausage or veggie. Since 2018, cult following. Near I-90 via Russell Street. Price: $8-12. Fun fact: Locals joke it's better than therapy.

5. **Market on Front** - Breakfast burritos dominate threads. Steak or veggie wraps with fresh salsa. X: "Top burrito ever." Downtown staple since 2014. Off I-90 via Front Street. Price: $10-14. Wrap-up: Portable perfection for the road.
