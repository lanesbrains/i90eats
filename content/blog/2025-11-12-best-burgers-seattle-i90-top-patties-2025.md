## Top Burger Places in Seattle

1. **Rain City Burgers** - Juicy smash burgers with caramelized onions. Reddit: "Best in town." Near I-90 in SODO. Price: $12-16. Dry humor: Wetter than Seattle rain, but tastier.

2. **Uneeda Burger** - Gourmet builds with house pickles. Eater: "Unbeatable." Fremont spot. Price: $13-17. Fun fact: Veggie options for green thumbs. [Link](https://uneedaburger.com/)

3. **Mean Sandwich** - Bold flavors like corned beef hybrids. Reddit: "Flavor bomb." Ballard area. Price: $14-18. Chuckle: Meaner than rush hour, but kinder to your taste buds.

4. **FlintCreek Cattle Co** - Grass-fed with upscale twists. Eater: "Elevated eats." Greenwood. Price: $15-19.

5. **Fam House** - Family-style with Asian fusions. Reviews: "Home run." Price: $12-16. Wrap-up: Patty power.
